1. <strong>
    • Used to indicate strong importance or emphasis.
    • Typically renders text in a bold or bolder font weight.
    • Best practice is to reserve its use for semantic emphasis rather than solely for styling purposes.
  2. <em>
    • Used to indicate emphasis on text.
    • Typically renders text in italics or with a slight change in styling.
    • Best practice is to use it for semantic emphasis rather than solely for styling purposes.
