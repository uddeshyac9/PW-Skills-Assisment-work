Benefits of using semantic tags in webpages include:

  1. Improved accessibility: Semantic tags provide clear structure and meaning to assistive technologies, making web content more accessible to people with disabilities.

  2. Enhanced search engine optimization (SEO): Search engines can better understand the content and hierarchy of a webpage when semantic tags are used, potentially improving its visibility in search results.

  3. Easier maintenance and development: Semantic tags make the code more readable and maintainable, allowing developers to easily understand and modify the structure of the webpage.

  4. Future-proofing: By using semantic tags, your webpage is more likely to be compatible with future standards and technologies, as they are designed to convey specific meanings and intentions.
